Setup

You will need: the Leap Motion cradle, track, velcro strips, Leap Motion USB cable, USB extender, the Leap Motion controller, and masking tape
  • You would use the tape for alignment. It is recommended that the tape be placed 2cm from the Rift's base.
  • You would place another piece of tape to mark the center-point
  • You will place the track above the tape from step one, and in the middle of the mark from step.
  • Remove tape once track is placed
  • Attach Oculus into the cradle and place on the track
  • Insert the small cable into the Leap Motion controller, make sure to secure cord onto the Rift's headset. Connect the USB extender to end of Leap Motion cable and attach to PC
  • After installing the proper drivers enable Leap Motion through the Oculus app
